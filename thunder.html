THUNDER

FITNESS ROUTINE TIMER WEB APP

As someone who takes his physical fitness very seriously, I am constantly trying new fitness applications to supplement my training. The current ecosystem on mobile app stores is trending towards specialised applications, aimed at solving one problem and making a lot of decisions on behalf of the user, for example cardio apps with preset runs, or lifting apps with predefined regimens.

While these are great for beginners, changing the options within them can difficult when the time comes to develop a tailored workout plan. This can be a result of limited choices or a bloated application that makes operating it confusing. Because of this, I wanted to develop a simple tool that the user could utilise however they wished.

Thunder lets a user create a custom workout of any kind. They can then use the timer to keep track of their workout. This allows a wider variety of possibilities than currently available in fitness applications, while keeping the operation of the app simple enough that it is convenient to use. One example is creating a 5km high intensity training run. Along side this the user can create a 30 minute stretch routine for flexibility. Users can even enter exercise classes such as Zumba or Insanity to take with them and tailor to their own needs.

The application is split into two halves; the back-end has been developed using the Django Rest-Framework, and the front-end has been created with the Angular.js framework. While I feel the app has been a successful proof of concept, there are still many features I’d like to add before I would consider it a finished product. I would also like to create a native iOS application, as I believe a native app would provide a higher quality experience that can’t be matched by a web application. Additionally, it would give me the opportunity to learn how to develop on a new platform that I am interested in.
